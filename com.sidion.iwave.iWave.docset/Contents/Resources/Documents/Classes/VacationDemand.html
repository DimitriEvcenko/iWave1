Overview

This class handles the vacation demands in core data.

Vacation Demands are deleted on logout or determination of the application. Only local demands that are not sent to wave intranet are persisted. Normaly a logged in User creates a new vacation demand an sends it to wave intranet. He only sees his vascation demands. Managers also can see the demands of direct or other reports.
