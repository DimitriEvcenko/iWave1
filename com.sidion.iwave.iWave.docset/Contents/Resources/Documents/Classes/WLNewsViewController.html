Overview

completeAnimationToRight:

This methods checks if a thresholdvalue on moving the news view is achieved. If it is the method finishes a started animation. Otherwise it removes the news view to origin.

- (void)completeAnimationToRight:(bool)right

Parameters

right

Yes if the animation should finish to the rigth side.

Discussion

This methods checks if a thresholdvalue on moving the news view is achieved. If it is the method finishes a started animation. Otherwise it removes the news view to origin.

The threshold value is achieved, when the collectionView is moved half of the news views width.
